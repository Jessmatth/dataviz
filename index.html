Purpose and Dataset Overview

This analysis was built to provide shareholders, executives, and the general public with insight into the often opaque subject of executive compensation. This data contains all 11,000 publicly-traded companies compensation as disclosed in records filed with the SEC between 2006-2021. The Yahoo Finance and Genderize.io API were also used.
